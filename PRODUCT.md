# PRODUCT.md — SAMMIE / The Marketing Pulse Summit

**register: brand**

## Product purpose
Marketing website for **The Marketing Pulse Summit**, the flagship in-person conference from **SAMMIE
(Best Social Media Brands)** by **Social Samosa**, 7th edition. Theme: *"What Drives India's Marketing
Economy — Attention. Growth. Culture."* The site sells attendance: it must feel like a premium, high-stakes
industry event and convert senior marketers to register. The design IS the product here; there is no app to
serve, the page is the pitch.

## Users
India's senior marketing decision-makers: CMOs, marketing leaders, founders, agency heads, brand + growth
leads. Time-poor, design-literate, skeptical of hype. They attend to learn from category-definers, network
with peers, and be seen at the room that matters. They judge credibility in seconds from visual polish.

## Brand
- **Identity:** SAMMIE / Social Samosa. Signature **black + gold** system (champagne-gold on near-black),
  cinematic stage-lit atmosphere, uppercase editorial display type. Confident, premium, culturally plugged-in.
- **Personality:** authoritative but energetic; editorial, not corporate; tech-forward, not gimmicky.
- **Voice:** punchy, declarative, provocative headlines ("Brand vs Performance Is Dead. Now What?").
  Short. No filler. Speaks to insiders.

## Tone
Confident, sharp, a little bold. Big statements backed by substance. Never salesy-desperate, never bland
conference-boilerplate.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)
- Generic dark SaaS landing pages (neon-blue-on-black, glow gradients, glass cards).
- Corporate conference templates (stock hero, blue links, three identical icon cards).
- Wedding/luxury-gold cliché (ornate serif filigree, metallic gradient text).
- Crypto/web3 neon aesthetics.
The gold is champagne and restrained, the black is a warm tinted near-black, the type does the work.

## Strategic principles
1. **Cinematic, not decorated.** Depth from real event imagery + lighting, not effects.
2. **Editorial hierarchy.** Big type, generous space, strong contrast. Copy is opinionated.
3. **Motion with intent.** Reveal, count-up, parallax, magnetic CTAs — purposeful, ease-out, reduced-motion safe.
4. **Convert relentlessly.** Register CTA always within reach; stats and speakers build credibility fast.
5. **Content is pending.** Structure everything as typed, swappable placeholder data (CMS-ready).
